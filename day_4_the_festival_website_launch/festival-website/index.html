About the Festival

Welcome to the Annual Winter Festival!

Every year, our town transforms into a snowy wonderland filled with laughter, creativity, and community spirit. From mystical fortune telling to heartwarming storytelling and the famous Hot Cocoa Championship, the Winter Festival brings together people of all ages for a full day of joy and connection.

  • Live performances
  • Seasonal treats and warm drinks
  • A beautifully decorated winter village
